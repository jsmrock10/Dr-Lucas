## Operations Plan

### Production Partners
1. **Local Master Atelier (Chicago)**
   - Capacity: 25 suits/month
   - Specialties: Hand-canvassed construction, custom linings
   - Lead Time: 6-8 weeks

2. **European Mill Partner (Milan)**
   - Capacity: 40 suits/month
   - Specialties: Performance fabrics, travel features
   - Lead Time: 3-4 weeks

### Quality Control
- 100% inspection rate
- Target 2% return rate
- 98% customer satisfaction goal
- 95% on-time delivery target

### Fulfillment Process
1. **Consultation (90 minutes):** Story discovery, measurements, style preferences
2. **Design Phase (2-3 days):** Fabric selection, motif design, approval
3. **Production (4-6 weeks):** Pattern creation, cutting, construction, lining integration
4. **Fittings (3 sessions):** Basting, forward, final fittings
5. **Delivery:** White-glove delivery with care instructions
